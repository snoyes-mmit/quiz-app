Frontend Developer Quiz

Quick, clear steps to help you finish your assessment with confidence.

Follow these simple instructions to prepare, navigate, and submit your quiz. Use the checklist below to keep yourself on track.

Choose the Right Quiz

Each quiz is designed to match where employees are in their onboarding journey, ensuring they’re tested on the right skills at the right time. Select the phase that best aligns with the employee’s experience level and the type of tasks you want to evaluate.

Phase 1 Quiz
Core, Everyday Tasks

This quiz focuses on the essential tasks performed consistently as part of day-to-day responsibilities. It ensures employees have mastered the fundamentals they’ll rely on every single day.

Phase 2 Quiz
Frequent, On-the-Job Tasks

This quiz evaluates tasks that are performed regularly—but may require more judgment, efficiency, or context than basic daily activities.

Phase 3 Quiz
Advanced, Complex Tasks

This quiz focuses on higher-level tasks that require deeper understanding, problem-solving, and confidence in the role.

Getting Started

Set up your environment and understand the rules before you begin.

1. Prepare Your Space
  • 1

    Choose a quiet place

    Find a distraction-free space with reliable internet access and a charged device.

  • 2

    Gather materials

    Have any allowed notes, calculators, or reference materials ready before you begin.

  • 3

    Check technical requirements

    Ensure your browser is up-to-date, pop-ups are allowed, and your webcam/microphone (if required) are working.

2. Review the Instructions
  • A

    Read the quiz guidelines

    Look for any time limits, question types, and rules about materials you can use.

  • B

    Know the scoring

    Understand whether there are penalties for wrong answers and how many points each question is worth.

  • C

    Locate navigation controls

    Familiarize yourself with buttons for next/previous, flagging questions, and submitting the quiz.

Pro Tips for Success

Small habits that make a big difference.

Stay on Track

If you have a timer, monitor it regularly and pace yourself so you have time to review your answers.

Answer Strategically

Answer questions you know first. Flag anything you are unsure about and come back to it later.

Confirm Before Submit

Before you submit, make sure you have answered all required questions and reviewed flagged items.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common quiz-related concerns.

What if I lose my connection?

Your progress is usually saved automatically. If you get disconnected, reconnect and resume where you left off. Contact support if you cannot return to the quiz.

Can I go back to previous questions?

Most quizzes allow you to revisit earlier questions. Use the navigation buttons and flag questions to review them later.

Is there a time extension?

Any time extensions must be requested in advance. Check the quiz rules or contact your instructor for accommodations.
